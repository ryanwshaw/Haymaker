Hole-by-Hole Logging

Every shot. Every detail.

HomeCourse Hero guides you through each hole with a step-by-step flow — tee shot, approach, short game, and putting. No guesswork, no forgetting what happened three holes ago.

1

Drive or tee shot

Pick your club and log where it ended up — fairway, rough, bunker, or worse. Par 3s skip straight to green results.

2

Approach distance & club

Log your yardage and club. HomeCourse Hero auto-suggests based on your bag settings and builds approach stats over time.

3

Approach result

Where did your approach land? Green, short, long, left, right, or bunker — feeds directly into your GIR and miss patterns.

4

Short game & putting

If you missed the green: log your chip club. Then record putts, first putt distance, and your total score.

Virtual Caddie

Your data-driven caddie

Import your launch monitor data, and HomeCourse Hero builds your bag automatically. On the course, the Virtual Caddie recommends the right club for every hole — backed by your real distances and scoring history.

How it works

1

Import your distances

Upload a CSV from Trackman, GCQuad, Mevo+, or any launch monitor. The app recognizes your clubs and stores average carry yardages automatically.

2

Your bag fills itself

Driver, 3W, 2i, 7W, irons, wedges — whatever's in your CSV gets added to your bag with real yardages. No manual entry needed.

3

Caddie recommends on-course

On each hole, a popup shows 2-3 club options based on your yardages, scoring history, and best approach ranges. Tap to select, or dismiss.

4

Smarter over time

The more rounds you log, the better the caddie gets. It learns which clubs you score best with on each hole and which approach distances are your sweet spot.
