HOW IT WORKS

How Badges Work

Badges aren't just trophies — they're live moments captured during your round.

⚡

Real-Time Detection

Badges are earned in real-time as you score your round. Hit two birdies in a row? You'll know instantly.

🎉

Pop-Up Alerts

When a badge triggers, a pop-up appears on screen with the badge animation. Hard to miss — even the ones you'd rather not see.

📜

Full History

Every badge you've ever earned is tracked in your profile. View your full badge history with timestamps and counts.

📍

Hole & Course Tracked

Each badge records which hole and course it was earned on. Relive the glory — or the horror — in full context.
